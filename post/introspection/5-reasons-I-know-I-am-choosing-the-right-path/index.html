5 Reasons I Know I am Choosing the Right Path

Alright, let me take a step back for a minute. I’m telling myself that I can succeed at something so many others have failed at. What makes me think I can achieve victory in this expenditure? Not to mention the fact I plan to not spend money, to hide it from family and friends and therefore receive little to no support, and .

I would try to argue that this blog is for me. Well, it is. There’s really not much arguing to it. It is a blog for me. It’s is for me in the sense that I become happy when I help people with their problems and inspiring them to do amazing things.

I’m fairly certain I’m not the only one to start a blog with those intents. I’m sure plenty have failed. So what makes me think I will succeed? My childhood fantasies, my dedication proven through education and athletic abilities, my ability to focus on the task at hand, and my understanding that I will probably never get directly paid to write these.
